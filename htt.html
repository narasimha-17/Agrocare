About LungCare AI

LungCare AI is an intelligent medical assistant designed to predict the risk of lung cancer based on various health and lifestyle indicators. The system leverages **machine learning** to make quick, data-driven predictions that help users understand their potential risk levels.

How It Works

  • The model takes in 15 health-related parameters such as age, smoking habits, and fatigue.
  • Each input is numerically encoded and processed through a trained machine learning algorithm.
  • The system classifies the input as either High Risk or Low Risk for lung cancer.
  • All predictions are instantaneous and privacy-friendly — no data is stored or shared.

Our Mission

Our mission is to **assist early lung cancer detection using Artificial Intelligence**. Early identification plays a critical role in increasing recovery chances. By providing easy access to AI-powered predictions, LungCare AI aims to raise awareness and support preventive healthcare initiatives worldwide.
